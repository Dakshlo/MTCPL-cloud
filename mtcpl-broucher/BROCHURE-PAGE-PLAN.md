# MTCPL Company Profile — 40-Page Plan

> Master specification for the next edition of the Mateshwari Temples Construction Pvt. Ltd. company profile brochure. Hand this file to any AI session along with `DESIGN-SYSTEM.md` (for visual rules) and `SHOT-CHECKLIST.md` (for photo references). Together those three files contain everything needed to design or code the book.

---

## 1 · Format specs

- **Total pages:** 40 (divisible by 4, prints cleanly in 10 four-page signatures)
- **Trim size:** A4 portrait — 210 × 297 mm
- **Binding:** Perfect-bound (glued spine, printed spine)
- **Padding:** 18 mm top · 22 mm sides · 22 mm bottom
- **Page-number footer:** bottom-right, 8 pt Inter, format `08 / 40`
- **No page number** on Cover (01), Inside Cover (02), Section Dividers (07, 13, 19, 27, 35), or Back Cover (40)
- **Background:** cream `#f5eedd` on every page except full-bleed photo pages
- **Print:** A4 margin 0, color-adjust exact, background graphics ON

## 2 · Audience

Dual-audience document. Must satisfy both:
- **Temple trusts and devotional clients** — reverent, family-firm, dharmic voice
- **Tier-1 EPC contractors** (L&T, Ahluwalia Contracts, Tata Projects, Shapoorji Pallonji) and their procurement/QA/safety teams — capability dossier, technical credentials, compliance evidence

## 3 · Narrative arc — Five Acts

| Act | Pages | Length | Purpose |
|-----|-------|--------|---------|
| I — Arrival | 01–06 | 6 pp | Set tone; introduce |
| II — Founder, Heritage & Ram Mandir | 07–18 | 12 pp | Tribute to founder; Ram Mandir as life's-work climax |
| III — Capability Dossier | 19–26 | 8 pp | Procurement evidence; tender-ready |
| IV — Wider Portfolio | 27–34 | 8 pp | Other projects, breadth, geographic reach |
| V — People, Trust, Close | 35–40 | 6 pp | Leadership, testimonials, contact, back cover |

Each Act opens with a full-bleed photographic section divider page (07, 13 is sub-divider, 19, 27, 35).

## 4 · Founder note

**Mr. Mancharam Lohar (Founder) is no longer alive.** All references throughout the book treat him as the late Founder. The Act II memorial pages use birth–death years (Founder will supply birth year; death year confirmed 2024 — he saw Ram Mandir consecrated before passing). Tone is reverent and past-tense for him personally, present-tense for the legacy and the company. Leadership page (36) lists the two active MDs only; Mancharamji is honored in a small banded strip above the columns as Founder, with no phone listing.
