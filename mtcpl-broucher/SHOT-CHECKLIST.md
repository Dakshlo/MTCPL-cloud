# MTCPL Plant Photo Shoot — Shot Checklist

**Total: 50 shots · single day · Sirohi yard**

---

## Cameraman essentials (read first)

- **Files:** Shoot RAW + JPEG, full resolution. Hand over on a hard drive at end of day.
- **No flash.** Natural light only — shed light, window light, outdoor light.
- **Run the machines.** Stone dust, water spray, motion = lively photos. Cold static machines look dead.
- **Shoot wide + mid + close** of every subject. Always one extra frame than you think you need.
- **Kit:** DSLR/mirrorless body · 24mm + 35mm + 50mm + 85mm primes (or 24–70 zoom + 85 prime) · macro for tool detail · tripod (mandatory for low-light interiors) · drone (Mavic 3 / Air 3 class) · reflector.
- **No phones, no people on phones, no logos of other brands visible in frame.**
